Privacy Policy

AvaWorld
Last updated: February 2026

VTA GOODS LLC ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") operates the mobile game AvaWorld (the "Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our Game.

By using AvaWorld, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Children's Privacy (COPPA / Designed for Kids)

AvaWorld is intended for children under the age of 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable laws.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information from children, such as real names, home addresses, or precise location.

The Game includes a parental gate to prevent children from accessing restricted features.

If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us at:
support@vtagoods.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect limited data necessary to operate the Game.

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Username / Nickname
  • Unique User ID

This information is used only to create and maintain the user account.

Device & Technical Information

  • Device ID
  • Advertising ID (for non-personalized ads)
  • Device model and operating system
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

Gameplay Information

  • Game progress and save data
  • Virtual currency and in-game purchases

Location

  • Approximate location (country/region level only)

We do not collect precise GPS location.

We DO NOT collect

  • Real name
  • Photos or media
  • Microphone or voice data
  • Chat messages
  • Contacts
  • Precise location

3. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and operate the Game
  • Save player progress (cloud save)
  • Maintain user accounts
  • Process in-app purchases via Apple / Google
  • Improve game stability and fix bugs
  • Show non-personalized advertisements

4. Advertising

AvaWorld uses Google AdMob to display non-personalized ads only.

Non-personalized ads do not use behavioral tracking and are suitable for child-directed apps.

AdMob may use:

  • Advertising ID
  • Basic device data
  • Contextual information

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. In-App Purchases

The Game offers optional in-app purchases processed securely by:

  • Apple App Store
  • Google Play

We do not store payment details such as credit card numbers.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share limited data only with:

  • Advertising provider (AdMob)
  • Cloud service provider (PlayFab)

Only the minimum necessary data is shared.

7. Data Storage & Retention

Data is stored:

  • Locally on the device
  • On secure PlayFab servers

Data is retained as long as the account exists, unless deletion is requested or required by law.

8. Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time by contacting:

support@vtagoods.com

Upon verification, account data including game progress and stored data will be permanently deleted from our systems, unless retention is required for legal or security purposes.

9. User Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request information about how your data is processed

To submit a request, contact:
support@vtagoods.com

10. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including:

  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Access controls
  • Encrypted communications where applicable

However, no system is completely secure.

11. Third-Party Logins

The Game may allow login via:

  • Google
  • Apple

These services may provide basic account information (such as email and ID) to enable authentication.

12. International Data Transfers

VTA GOODS LLC is registered in the United States. Data may be processed in the USA and other countries where our service providers operate.

We apply appropriate safeguards where required by law.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised "Last updated" date.

Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

VTA GOODS LLC
Email: support@vtagoods.com
